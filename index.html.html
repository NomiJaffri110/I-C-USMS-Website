About Us

Welcome to Our Department

The Department of Information and Computing at USMS-Bhitshah is committed to providing high-quality education in the fields of Information Technology and Computer Science. Our programs are designed to equip students with the technical skills and theoretical knowledge needed to excel in the rapidly evolving tech industry.

With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and a curriculum aligned with industry needs, we prepare our graduates to become leaders in technology and innovation.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in computing education and research that produces technologically competent and ethically responsible professionals.

Our Mission

To provide quality education through innovative teaching methods, cutting-edge research, and industry collaborations that address societal challenges.

Programs Offered

BS Information Technology

The BS IT program focuses on the application of computing technology to solve business and organizational problems. Students gain expertise in:

  • Software Development
  • Database Systems
  • Network Administration
  • Web Technologies
  • IT Project Management
Learn More

BS Computer Science

The BS CS program provides a strong foundation in computer science principles and practices, with emphasis on:

  • Algorithms and Data Structures
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Computer Systems
  • Software Engineering
  • Theoretical Foundations

Chairperson's Message

Prof. Dr. Muhammad Qasim Memon

Chairperson, Department of Information and Computing

Dear Students and Visitors,

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome you to the Department of Information and Computing at the University of Sufism and Modern Sciences, Bhitshah. In this era of digital transformation, our department stands at the forefront of technological education and innovation.

Our programs are carefully designed to equip students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills needed to excel in the rapidly evolving field of computing. We emphasize hands-on learning through our state-of-the-art labs, research projects, and industry collaborations.

Our faculty members are dedicated to nurturing talent and fostering an environment of creativity and critical thinking. We take pride in our graduates who are making significant contributions in various sectors locally and internationally.

I invite you to explore our programs and discover the opportunities that await you at our department. Together, let's shape the future of technology.

Warm regards,

Prof. Dr. Muhammad Ali
